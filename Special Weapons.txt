--------------------------------------------------
Introducing the new attack Type: Darkness (Dk). Attacks with type Darkness drains a percentage of the enemy's current LP when hit. Darkness attacks of AT X%-Y% will drain X% to Y% of current LP from the enemy. AT percentage supports decimals. All non-bosses do not have Darkness resistance and all bosses have 80% Darkness resistance, reducing the percentages by 80%. Darkness damages CANNOT be improved by using AT Aura caused by Priests and/or the Berserk Card if the damage comes from BAT. In addition, Darkness weapons cannot be equipped with Magic Amp's Card even if they require MP.

This set of weapons, when equipped, turns the stickman's head and body purple.
