Blue Crystal
--------------------------------------------------
A Crystal specialized for Wizards, which increases Max Mana. LV1-5 Buyable for $1200xLV (LV2-5 only available at Island), sells for $150xLV.
Restrictions: Tomes.
Blue Crystal 1: Max Mana +100
Blue Crystal 2: Max Mana +200
Blue Crystal 3: Max Mana +300
Blue Crystal 4: Max Mana +400
Blue Crystal 5: Max Mana +500
Blue Crystal 6: Max Mana +600
Blue Crystal 7: Max Mana +800
Blue Crystal 8: Max Mana +1000

Increases Max Mana of the Wizard, which both allows the Wizard to cast more attacks before running out of Mana, and also indirectly increases the %-based Mana Regeneration if DEX was invested.

Spinel
--------------------------------------------------
New Jewel which increases the burn rate of fire attacks. LV 1-5 buyable for $2000xLV (LV2-5 only available at Island)
Restrictions: Weapons of type Fire.
Spinel 1: 15% Higher Fire Burn Rate
Spinel 2: 30% Higher Fire Burn Rate
Spinel 3: 45% Higher Fire Burn Rate
Spinel 4: 60% Higher Fire Burn Rate
Spinel 5: 75% Higher Fire Burn Rate
Spinel 6: 90% Higher Fire Burn Rate
Spinel 7: 105% Higher Fire Burn Rate
Spinel 8: 120% Higher Fire Burn Rate
Increases the chance for fire attacks to deal damage each frame, or more commonly known as the burn rate. Equipping Spinels on explosion based weapons have no effect.

Cursed Gem

Cursed Gem 7: Darkness LP Damage Ratio +100%

Souls
--------------------------------------------------
New Compo Item set which gives characters a chance of creating bonus attacks when attacking. 10% for Gloves and Dual Swords. 20% for Swords and Whips, and 40% for Hammers. Only one Soul allowed per weapon.
Restrictions: Gloves, Swords, Whips, Dual Swords, Hammers. 
LV1-5 Buyable for $4000xLV (LV2-5 only available at Island) and sells for $500xLV.

Fire Souls:
LV1 (Blaze): 2 Fire falls from the sky to the ground. AT 4-5 (Burn 2%) and the fire lasts for 2s.
LV2 (Atomic Ray): 3 Lasers like those from the Fire Spirit 4 is spawned. AT 10-10 (Burn 5%). The lowered burn rate makes it difficult to deal damage to the same enemy multiple times due to its constant movement.
LV3 (Meteor): Spawns a Fire type version of the Ice Meteor. The meteor has AT of 8-8 (Burn 5%) and pierces through enemies. When it hits the ground 8 fire of AT 6-8 (Burn 5%) is spawned. Fire lasts for 1.2s. 
LV4 (Flame Trail): Spawns a large ball which constantly produces heat clouds (Constant 30) like the Charge Ring 2. Heat clouds have AT of 6-8 (Burn 5%). Effect lasts for 3s and each cloud lasts for 1s. 
LV5 (Flame Wall): Drops a harmless pellet that burst into 30 blaze attacks up the sky. AT 8-12 (Burn 4%), each blaze lasts for 2s.
LV6 (Ultra Burn): Spawns a large homing blaze attack that deals splash damage at an extreme rate. AT 10-15 (Burn 50%), the blaze lasts for 2s.
LV7 (Cosmic Ray): Spawns 2 lasers that works like the Wizard's Cosmic Beam 5, producing balls constantly (Constant 50). The lasers pierce terrain instead. Laser AT 8-16 (Burn 10%), deals splash damage and lasts for 3s. Ball residue AT 8-12 (Burn 10%) and lasts for 1s.
LV8 (Hell Fire): Spawns 25 homing clouds. AT 45-75 (Burn 5%), the clouds lasts for 4s.

Thunder Souls:
LV1 (Lightning): Spawns a thunder of AT 1-22.
LV2 (Great Shock): Spawns a large shock attack which deals splash damage. AT 1-33.
LV3 (Beam Strike): Spawns 10 lasers like those from the Thunder Chain 3. AT 1-44.
LV4 (Thunder Spike): Spawns 9 spikes like those from the Yellow Big Coconut Snake. AT 1-88.
LV5 (Thunder Cloud): Spawns a cloud that drops thunderbolts like the Charge Circle 4. AT 1-88 (Constant 15).
LV6 (Big Thunderstorm): Spawns 9 huge thunderbolts in the same fashion as the Thunder Storm 2. Thunderbolts deal splash damage. AT 1-222.
LV7 (Shocking Spark): Spawns 9 sparks which bounces off terrain and has 20 homing range. Sparks leave a shock attack that deals splash damage. AT 1-333, BAT 1-111.
LV8 (Shockwave): Spawns 36 waves like the Wizard's Shockwave 8 without residues. AT 1-666.

Ice Souls:
LV1 (Snowflake): Spawns a snowflake flying towards an enemy. AT 6-12, Slow 40%.
LV2 (Blizzard): Spawns 8 bolts in the same fashion as the Blizzard orb. AT 6-12, Slow 30%.
LV3 (Big Icicle): Spawns a big icicle like how the Thunder Spirit 3 summons a thunderbolt. Deals splash damage. AT 18-24, Slow 20%.
LV4 (Icicle Barrage): Spawns 10 needles in the same fashion as the Thunder orb. AT 24-36, Slow 30%.
LV5 (Ultra Slow): Spawns 3 snowflakes in the same fashion as the Blizzard orb. AT 100-150, Slow 60%.
LV6 (Ice Mist): Drops a harmless pellet which burst into 8 cloud attacks that does splash damage and slows. AT 64-96, Slow 30%.
LV7 (Ice Flare): Spawns 2 icicles like the Ice Soul 4. When the icicles hit an enemy it burst into 3 blazes which deal splash damage. AT 480-640, BAT 64-96, Slow 35%.
LV8 (Quad Ice Meteor): Spawns 4 meteors in the same fashion as Ice Soul 2 and 5. Upon collision the meteors burst into 9 bolts. AT 900-1350, BAT 100-150, Slow 40%.

Dusts
--------------------------------------------------
New Compo Item set based on HankGuideDude's idea on FB Wiki. Equipping a Dust allows characters to produce attacks at a constant rate unless if the character is dragged or if there are no enemies. Some parameters of the compo family were tweaked for game balancing. Only one Dust allowed per weapon.

Restrictions: 
LV1-6 Buyable for $2000xLV (LV2-6 only available at Island), sells for $250xLV. 
Attack Modes:
LV1: Radially away from characters.
LV2: Dropped around characters like the Blizzard 2/Thunder Storm 2.
LV3: 3 projectiles at fixed angles above characters like the Ice Castle Boss's Spear attack. Affected by gravity.
LV4: 4 projectiles radially away from charcters at 90 degrees from each other. 
LV5: Same as LV2 but with increased range.
LV6: 2 projectiles radially away from charcters at 180 degrees from each other. 
LV7: 7 projectiles burst upwards from charcters. Affected by gravity.
LV8: Same as LV1.
