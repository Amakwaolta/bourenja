Flare's Card
--------------------------------------------------
New card which increases the amount of residue attacks.
Restrictions: Weapons with magical attacks that produces more than 1 residue or produces residues at a rate, Swords, Dual Swords and Hammers which releases magical attacks one by one/set by set.
LV1-5 Buyable for $4000xLV (LV2-5 only available at Island), sells for $500xLV.
Flare's Card 1: +20% Residue Count/Production
Flare's Card 2: +40% Residue Count/Production
Flare's Card 3: +60% Residue Count/Production
Flare's Card 4: +80% Residue Count/Production
Flare's Card 5: +100% Residue Count/Production
Flare's Card 6: +120% Residue Count/Production
Flare's Card 7: +150% Residue Count/Production
Flare's Card 8: +200% Residue Count/Production
Equipping a high LV Flare's Card on certain weapons will also increase the spread of the residues. The list includes Ice Meteor 4, Volcano 2, Super Volcano 4, Time Explosion 5 and Napalm Bomb 6 (and possibly more). Equipping a high LV Flare's Card on Ice Meteor 4 may be counterproductive as the great spread of projectiles makes it much easier to miss due the the limited lifespan of residues and reduced accuracy. However doing so on Super Volcano 4 gives a potential for the flames to hit even flying enemies.

Slider's Card
--------------------------------------------------
New card which allows projectiles to slide on terrain.
Restrictions: Bows, Orbs, Guns, Whips, Rings, Tomes
LV1-5 Buyable for $4000xLV (LV2-5 only available at Island), sells for $500xLV.
Slider's Card 1: 25% chance Terrain Sliding
Slider's Card 2: 50% chance Terrain Sliding
Slider's Card 3: 75% chance Terrain Sliding
Slider's Card 4: 100% chance Terrain Sliding
Slider's Card 5: 100% chance Terrain Sliding, Projectile/Residue Lifespan +12.5%
Slider's Card 6: 100% chance Terrain Sliding, Projectile/Residue Lifespan +25%
Slider's Card 7: 100% chance Terrain Sliding, Projectile/Residue Lifespan +37.5%
Slider's Card 8: 100% chance Terrain Sliding, Projectile/Residue Lifespan +50%
This card can change projectiles with reflecting capabilities to have sliding capabilities, but if a Reflection Card is also equipped the Reflection Card takes precedence and makes projectiles reflect off terrain. Sliding capability has no effect if the projectile also has piercing capabilities. Projectile lifespan increment works like the Extension's Card.

Restrictions: Weapons which create projectiles.

Extension's Card 1: Projectile/Residue Lifespan +12.5%
Extension's Card 2: Projectile/Residue Lifespan +25%

Extension's Card 7: Projectile/Residue Lifespan +90%

This card is useless on many weapons where their projectiles usually hit the enemies immediately or disappears when hitting the terrain. New Card which gives a massive boost to the AT of Bonus attacks at the expense of increased MP requirement.
Restrictions: Weapons which have MP-based magical attacks.
Sells for $625xLV. Only even LVs are available as a way to limit its flexibility.
Magic Amp's Card 2: +100% MP, +200% Bonus AT
Magic Amp's Card 4: +200% MP, +400% Bonus AT
Magic Amp's Card 6: +300% MP, +600% Bonus AT
Magic Amp's Card 8: +400% MP, +800% Bonus AT
Highly useful on eligible characters having high MAG wielding weapons which have a relatively low MP cost, or weapons which have prolonged effects such as type Poison weapons. Also provides a whole new set of possible builds to eligible classes.

Focus's Card
--------------------------------------------------
New Card which focuses projectiles.
Restrictions: Bows, Orbs, Guns, Whips, Blowguns and Tomes which fire more than 1 projectile using specific attack modes. (See list below)
Sells for $625xLV. Only LVs 3, 5 and 7 are available.
Focus's Card 3: Focused Projectiles
Focus's Card 5: Focused Projectiles, +50% Projectile Count
Focus's Card 7: Focused Projectiles, +75% Projectile Count
Makes some weapons with great spread deal focused damage. Focusing effect is useless if the weapon already has focused attacks like the Thunder Spear 4. Projectile increment on this card is calculated before the Bullet's Card.

Satan's Card
--------------------------------------------------
The card that has been hidden within the old SR versions is now alive! Has the power to insta-kill any non-boss enemy. Incompatible with Souls and the Devil's Card. Sells for $5000.
Restrictions: Gloves, Swords, Whips, Dual Swords, Hammers. 
Satan's Card 6: 2%/4%/8% chance Instant Death
2% chance for Gloves and Dual Swords. 4% chance for Swords and Whips. 8% chance for Hammers. This card makes characters able to instantly kill any non-boss enemy with a low chance. When the effect is triggered a large red shuriken appears which deals 9999999 damage to the enemy. The damage is shown in dark red numbers. All bosses are immune to this attack and will only take 1 damage from this attack.

Devil's Card
--------------------------------------------------
Another card that has been hidden within the old SR versions is now alive! Has the power to deal extra damage which is proportional to the enemy's current LP. Incompatible with Souls and the Satan's Card. Sells for $3000.
Restrictions: Gloves, Swords, Whips, Dual Swords, Hammers. 
Devil's Card 4: 5%/10%/20% chance 30% LP Damage
5% chance for Gloves and Dual Swords. 10% chance for Swords and Whips. 20% chance for Hammers. This card makes characters able to deal extra damage to enemies based on the enemy's current LP. When the effect is triggered a large purple shuriken appears which removes 30% of the enemy's current LP. The damage is shown in purple numbers. All bosses are resistant to this attack and will only lose 6% of its current LP from this attack.
